How do I count Food Guide Servings in a meal?

Vegetable and beef stir-fry with rice,a glass of milk and an apple for dessert
250mL(1 cup) mixed brocoli, carrot and sweet red pepper = 2 Vegatables and Fruit Food Guide Servings
75g(2oz.) lean beef = 1 Meat and Alternatives Food Guide Serving
250mL(1cup) brown rice = 2Grain Products Food Guide Servings
5mL(1tsp) canola oil = part of your Oils and Fats intake for the day
250mL(1cup) 1% milk = 1Milk and Alternatives Food Guide Serving
1 apple = 1Vegetables and Fruit Food Guide Serving
